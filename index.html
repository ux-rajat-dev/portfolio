Who Am I?

I'm Rajat Kumar Burde, a passionate and detail-oriented UI/UX Designer and Developer dedicated to crafting seamless digital experiences. With a solid foundation in design principles and technical expertise, I bridge the gap between creativity and functionality.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's of Design in User Interface and User Experience, I have honed my skills in creating user-centric designs and robust backend solutions. My journey includes a successful internship as a UI/UX Designer, where I delivered a client-approved Alumni Association project using a meticulous 5D design process.

I am also a certified Java Desktop Application Developer and proficient in backend development using ASP.NET (Web API) and PHP, alongside front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Driven by a love for problem-solvingand innovation, I strive to create designs and solutions that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and efficient. Whether collaborating on a team project or working independently, my goal is to deliver exceptional results that resonate with users and clients alike.

When I'm not designing or coding, you can find me exploring the latest trends in technology, enhancing my skills, or brainstorming new creative ideas.
